An opportunity for students to explore contemporary constitutional issues. It will encourage students to become better informed about the Australian system, of government and how the Australian Constitution provides a framework for our democracy.

Interested students should submit an Expression of Interest that includes; a 500 word statement on how attending the convention will benefit them and their school and a 2 page resume.

Applications should be sent to curriculum@education.tas.gov.au

Intended Audience: Students who will be in Year 12 in 2016
